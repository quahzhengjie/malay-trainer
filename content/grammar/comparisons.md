Malay comparisons use a small set of fixed patterns.

| Pattern | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| lebih ... daripada | more ... than | Ali lebih tinggi daripada Ahmad. |
| kurang ... | less ... | Teh ini kurang manis. |
| paling ... | the most ... | Ini yang paling murah. |
| ter- (prefix) | the most ... | Ini termurah. |
| sama ... dengan | as ... as | Dia sama tinggi dengan saya. |

`paling murah` and `termurah` mean the same thing — "cheapest". `paling` is a
separate word placed before the adjective; `ter-` is a prefix stuck onto it.
