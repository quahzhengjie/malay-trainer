Particles are tiny words that add nuance without changing the basic meaning.

**`-lah`** — softens or emphasises. Attached to the key word.

- *Pergilah!* — Do go! / Go on, then.
- *Sikit sahaja-lah.* — Just a little, then.

**`-kah`** — marks a question (common in writing).

- *Betulkah?* — Is that right?

**`pun`** — "also" or "even".

- *Saya pun tahu.* — I know too.

**`-nya`** — works as "the / its / his", or turns a phrase into an exclamation.

- *rumahnya* — his house / the house
- *Cantiknya!* — How pretty!
