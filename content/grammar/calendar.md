## Days of the week

| Malay | English |
|---|---|
| Isnin | Monday |
| Selasa | Tuesday |
| Rabu | Wednesday |
| Khamis | Thursday |
| Jumaat | Friday |
| Sabtu | Saturday |
| Ahad | Sunday |

*Hari apa hari ini?* — What day is it today?

## When

**hari ini** today · **semalam** yesterday · **esok** tomorrow ·
**lusa** the day after tomorrow · **minggu** week · **bulan** month · **tahun** year.

## Months

Januari · Februari · **Mac** · April · **Mei** · **Jun** · **Julai** · **Ogos** ·
September · Oktober · November · Disember.

A date reads day then month: **1 Mei** — the first of May. The word *haribulan*
means "date (of the month)".
