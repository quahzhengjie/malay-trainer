---
id: memper-prefix
title: The memper- prefix
level: C1
tags: [affix]
---
**`memper-`** is a layered prefix that forms transitive verbs, usually with a
causative or intensifying sense. It is a hallmark of careful, formal writing.

**With an adjective — to intensify or increase:**

- besar → **memperbesar** (to enlarge)
- dalam → **memperdalam** (to deepen)

**With a noun or verb root — to cause, or to treat as:**

- kenal → **memperkenalkan** (to introduce)
- oleh → **memperoleh** (to obtain)

`memper-` often combines with `-kan` or `-i` — *memperkenalkan*, *memperbaiki*
(to repair). Do not confuse it with plain `me-`: *membesar* means "to grow
bigger", but *memperbesar* means "to make something bigger".
