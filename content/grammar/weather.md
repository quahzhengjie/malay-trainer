*Bagaimana cuaca hari ini?* — What is the weather like today?

| Malay | English |
|---|---|
| cuaca | weather |
| panas | hot |
| sejuk | cold |
| hujan | rain |
| cerah | clear, sunny |
| mendung | cloudy |
| angin | wind |
| ribut | storm |

- **Hari ini panas.** — It is hot today.
- **Hujan turun.** — It is raining. (*turun* = to come down)
- **panas terik** — scorching hot.

Malaysia has no four seasons — mainly a **musim hujan** (rainy season) and a
drier spell. *musim* means "season".
