title: The -i suffix
level: B1
tags: [affix, verbs]
---
The **`-i`** suffix usually means the object is a **place or surface**, or that
the action is done repeatedly or thoroughly.

| Root | Verb | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| duduk | menduduki | to occupy / sit on |
| tanam | menanami | to plant on (an area) |
| kunjung | mengunjungi | to visit (a person or place) |
| masuk | memasuki | to enter (a place) |

Compare `-kan` and `-i` with the same root **masuk**:

- *memasukkan* — to **put** something **in** (it moves the object)
- *memasuki* — to **enter** a place (it acts on the place)

Rule of thumb: **`-kan` moves something; `-i` acts on a place.**
