title: Making nouns
level: B2

Circumfixes — affixes wrapped around both ends of a root — turn verbs and
adjectives into nouns.

**`ke-...-an`** — abstract nouns:

- cantik → **kecantikan** (beauty)
- selamat → **keselamatan** (safety)

It also forms "adversative" nouns — being caught by something:

- hujan → **kehujanan** (caught in the rain)

**`pe-...-an`** — a process or its result, built from `me-` verbs:

- bangun → **pembangunan** (development)
- didik → **pendidikan** (education)

**`per-...-an`** — built from `ber-` verbs:

- kahwin → **perkahwinan** (marriage)

And **`pe-`** on its own marks the *doer*: **pelajar** (student),
**pekerja** (worker), **penulis** (writer).
