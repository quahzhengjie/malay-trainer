The key verb is **`pesan`** (to order); in everyday speech **`nak`** ("want")
does most of the work.

| Malay | English |
|---|---|
| menu | menu |
| makanan | food |
| minuman | drinks |
| pesan | to order |
| sedap | delicious |
| pedas | spicy |
| bungkus | to pack / takeaway |
| bil | the bill |

Useful lines:

- **Saya nak pesan nasi goreng.** — I'd like to order fried rice.
- **Kurang pedas, ya.** — Less spicy, please.
- **Air kosong** — plain water · **tanpa gula** — without sugar.
- **Makan sini** — eat in · **bungkus** — takeaway.
- **Tolong bawa bil.** — The bill, please.
