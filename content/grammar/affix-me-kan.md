title: The -kan suffix
level: B1
tags: [affix, verbs]
---
Adding **`-kan`** to a verb (usually alongside `me-`) gives it one of two jobs.

**1. Causative** — to *make* something happen.

| Root | Verb | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| bersih | membersihkan | to clean (make clean) |
| jelas | menjelaskan | to explain (make clear) |
| betul | membetulkan | to correct / fix |

**2. Benefactive** — to do something *for* someone.

| Root | Verb | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| beli | membelikan | to buy (for someone) |
| buat | membuatkan | to make (for someone) |

So *membersihkan bilik* = clean the room; *membelikan adik baju* = buy little
sibling a shirt.
