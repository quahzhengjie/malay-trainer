Malaysia's currency is the **ringgit** (RM); 100 **sen** make one ringgit.

| Malay | English |
|---|---|
| Berapa harga ini? | How much is this? |
| mahal | expensive |
| murah | cheap |
| duit / wang | money |
| beli | to buy |
| jual | to sell |
| bayar | to pay |
| baki | change (money returned) |

Useful lines when shopping:

- **Saya nak beli ini.** — I want to buy this.
- **Terlalu mahal!** — Too expensive!
- **Boleh kurang?** — Can you lower the price? (bargaining)
- **Ada yang lebih murah?** — Is there a cheaper one?
