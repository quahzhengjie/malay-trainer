**Simpulan bahasa** are short fixed idioms — usually two words — whose meaning
cannot be guessed from the parts.

| Idiom | Literally | Means |
|---|---|---|
| buah tangan | fruit of the hand | a gift / souvenir |
| buah hati | fruit of the heart | a sweetheart |
| makan angin | to eat wind | to go on a leisure trip |
| naik darah | blood rises | to become angry |
| ringan tulang | light bones | hardworking, diligent |
| berat tulang | heavy bones | lazy |
| panjang tangan | long hands | prone to stealing |
| mulut murai | magpie mouth | very talkative |

Knowing these is the difference between sounding fluent and sounding like a
textbook.
