Malay has two registers, and switching between them is a real skill.

- **Bahasa baku** — standard, formal. Used in writing, news, exams, speeches.
- **Bahasa percakapan** — everyday spoken Malay.

| Formal (baku) | Colloquial | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| tidak | tak | not |
| hendak | nak | want to |
| sahaja | saja / je | only, just |
| tetapi | tapi | but |
| kerana | sebab | because |
| anda / awak | kau | you |

Speech also sprinkles in the **`-lah`** particle (*makanlah!*) and drops words
that writing keeps. Write in baku; speak however your friends do.
