Repeating a word — **reduplication** — does several different jobs in Malay.

| Type | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Plurality | buku-buku | books (many) |
| Variety | sayur-sayuran | all sorts of vegetables |
| Casualness | jalan-jalan | to stroll, to wander about |
| Intensity | cantik-cantik | all very pretty |
| Reciprocity | tolong-menolong | to help one another |

Beware: some reduplicated words are **fixed** and signal none of the above.
*kanak-kanak* simply means "child", and *mata-mata* means "police officer" —
neither is a plural.
