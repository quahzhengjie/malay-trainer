Formal and written Malay uses a richer set of connectors than everyday speech.

| Connector | Meaning | Note |
|---|---|---|
| namun | however | formal form of *tetapi* |
| oleh itu | therefore | formal form of *jadi* |
| manakala | whereas, while | contrasts two clauses |
| justeru | hence, precisely | adds weight to a consequence |
| sungguhpun | even though | formal form of *walaupun* |
| lantaran | because of | formal, literary form of *kerana* |
| demi | for the sake of | states a purpose or motive |

These belong in essays, news and speeches. Using *tapi* or *sebab* in that
setting sounds too casual.
