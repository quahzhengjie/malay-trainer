id: relative-yang
title: The word yang
level: B1
tags: [grammar]
---
**`yang`** links a noun to a description — it does the job of English
"that", "which" and "who".

| Malay | English |
|---|---|
| orang yang tinggi | the person **who** is tall |
| buku yang saya beli | the book **that** I bought |
| rumah yang besar | the house **which** is big |

`yang` can also stand on its own to mean "the ... one":

- **yang merah** — the red one
- **yang itu** — that one

The pattern is always: *noun* + **yang** + *the description*.
