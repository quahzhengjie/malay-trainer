id: prefix-ter
title: The ter- prefix
level: B1
tags: [affix]
---
The **`ter-`** prefix has three distinct jobs. Context tells you which one is meant.

**1. Accidental** — something happened unintentionally.

- jatuh → **terjatuh** (fell by accident)
- lupa → **terlupa** (forgot)

**2. Superlative** — the most.

- besar → **terbesar** (biggest)
- baik → **terbaik** (best)

**3. Ability** — managed to / could (often with *tidak*).

- Beg itu **tidak terangkat** — that bag could not be lifted.

What `ter-` never does is mark tense — Malay has no verb tenses.
