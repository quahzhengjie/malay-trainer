**Peribahasa** are traditional Malay proverbs. They carry cultural wisdom and
appear in formal writing, speeches and literature.

| Proverb | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Bagai aur dengan tebing | mutual support and interdependence |
| Melentur buluh biarlah dari rebungnya | shape good habits in a child early |
| Seperti katak di bawah tempurung | narrow-minded; ignorant of the wider world |
| Bagai kacang lupakan kulit | ungrateful; forgetting one's origins |
| Ada udang di sebalik batu | there is a hidden motive |
| Sudah terhantuk baru terngadah | to realise something only when it is too late |
| Bagai pinang dibelah dua | a perfectly matched pair |

A well-placed peribahasa is a mark of true command of the language.
