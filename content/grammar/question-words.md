Malay has a small, fixed set of question words. In a simple question they
usually sit where the answer would go.

| Malay | Asks about | Example |
|---|---|---|
| apa | what | Apa ini? — What is this? |
| siapa | who | Siapa itu? — Who is that? |
| mana | where | Di mana? — Where? |
| bila | when | Bila? — When? |
| kenapa / mengapa | why | Kenapa? — Why? |
| berapa | how much / many | Berapa? — How much? |
| bagaimana | how | Bagaimana? — How? |

> You can also turn a statement into a yes/no question just by raising your
> intonation — no extra word needed. *Awak makan?* = "Are you eating?"
